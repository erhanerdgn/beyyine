Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Beyyine ("App") is developed by Erhan ERDOGAN ("Developer"). This privacy policy explains the information collected, used, and shared when you use the App.

1. Information Collected

1.1 Location Data

The App uses your device's location for:

  • Calculating prayer times
  • Determining Qibla direction
  • Showing nearby mosques

Location data is processed only on your device and is not sent to our servers.

1.2 Usage Data

Anonymous usage statistics and crash reports may be collected (via Sentry) to improve app quality. This data does not contain personal information.

1.3 Account Information

If you use Sign in with Apple or Google Sign-In, only your name and email address are stored for cloud sync purposes (via Supabase). This information is not shared with third parties.

2. Advertisements

The free version displays Google AdMob advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers for ad personalization. Ads are completely removed with a Premium subscription.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — Ad serving
  • Sentry — Error reporting
  • Supabase — Cloud storage and authentication
  • Apple Maps — Mosque finder and directions
  • Presidency of Religious Affairs (Diyanet) — Sermon content

4. Data Storage

Prayer times, preferences, and Quran reading progress are stored only on your device (encrypted storage). Cloud sync is optional and works only when you sign in.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion or export of your data via GitHub Issues.

7. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
