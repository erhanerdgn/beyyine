Frequently Asked Questions

Are the prayer times accurate?

Yes. Beyyine uses a calculation method compatible with the Turkish Presidency of Religious Affairs (Diyanet). Make sure your location permission is enabled.

What should I do if the Qibla compass is not working?

Calibrate your compass by moving your device in a figure-8 pattern. Stay away from magnetic field sources.

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

iPhone: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Beyyine > Cancel Subscription.

Can I request data deletion?

Yes. You can request data deletion via GitHub Issues.
